Round 1 — Wireframes

This is a picture of the redesign, not the build. No code has changed. Your signoff on this round starts the build rounds (per the wireframe-before-implementing discipline). Dated 2026-07-08.

YourCondoManager · Product Redesign · Private gated review

Round 1 — full-surface wireframe set

Fourteen frames covering all six zones of the manager web app, all four zones of the owner/board portal, and the portal at phone width — every frame on the real YCM brand (deep teal #014D4A, accent teal #15A39C, gold #f0b400, Inter Tight) and built against the actual product IA (the six-zone sidebar + four-zone portal shipped in Phase 11 / 3.5), not an invented one. Content uses a fictional but realistic Cherry Hill Court dataset — no real tenant data.

How the brand translates

  • Operator surfaces (manager app) get the deep-teal chrome — dense, high-trust, built for daily ops.
  • Owner-facing surfaces (portal) get a lighter, warmer shell — same palette, more air, first-person labels ("My Finances", "My Requests").
  • Gold is reserved for attention — balances due, flagged items, notifications — never decorative.
  • Inter Tight throughout; card radius + spacing rhythm held constant across every frame.

Frame inventory

  • Web (manager): 6 frames — one per sidebar zone (Home, Financials, Operations, Governance, Communications, Platform).
  • Portal (residents & board): 4 frames — one per portal zone (Home, My Finances, My Requests, My Community).
  • Mobile: 4 frames — the portal's real bottom-tab pattern at phone width.

What feedback is most useful now

  • Does the deep-teal operator chrome vs. the lighter portal shell read as one product, or too different?
  • Is the Financials Hub's Setup / Operations / Insight grouping clear at a glance?
  • Should Round 2 wireframe the remaining sub-pages (e.g. Billing detail, Work Order detail) at this same fidelity, or move straight to build on what's here?
