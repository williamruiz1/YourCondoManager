About our community

Who we are and how we run things

Cherry Hill Court is an 18-home condominium community in New Haven, Connecticut, owned and run by the people who live here.

Our volunteer board keeps the grounds maintained, the reserves healthy, and the community well-run — without a third-party management company. Dues are now collected securely online, so paying is simple and there's a clear record for every owner. This page is the single place to handle everything: pay what you owe, reach the board, find documents, and keep up with what's happening.

  • Owner-managed — decisions made by residents, for residents
  • Secure online dues with a receipt and full payment history
  • One portal for documents, requests, notices, and meetings
