About the community

Cherry Hill Court is an 18-residence condominium community in New Haven, Connecticut. Run by an elected board of resident owners, the community is self-managed — day-to-day operations, finances, and governance are handled directly by the board with the support of YourCondoManager.

Owners stay informed and take care of everything — dues, account statements, maintenance requests, and governing documents — through a single secure Owner Portal. Prospective buyers, lenders, and insurers can request documents and certificates through the community's official channels.

This page is the community's public front door. Owners should sign in to the Owner Portal for account-specific information; the contacts below are here for general inquiries.
