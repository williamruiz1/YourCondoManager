## The problem

Self-managed condo and HOA boards run a real business on spreadsheets and email. Volunteers — not accountants — juggle bank accounts, owner ledgers, vendor invoices, and compliance deadlines across a dozen disconnected tools.

- Dues and reserves tracked in spreadsheets that don't reconcile to the bank
- Owner balances and payment history scattered across email threads
- Vendor invoices and work orders with no single record
- Compliance and meeting deadlines that slip because nobody owns the calendar

## The solution

Your Condo Manager is the single-pane platform built for self-managed communities — finances, governance, residents, and maintenance in one place, with no expensive management fees.

- **Financials & banking** — Connect accounts with Plaid, track dues and reserves, and reconcile every transaction. No spreadsheets, no surprises at audit time.
- **Board & owner workflow** — Run meetings, votes, work orders, and document sharing in one place. Owners get a portal to view their account and pay dues.
- **Compliance & governance** — Store governing documents, track board decisions and minutes, and stay ahead of deadlines with built-in reminders.
- **AI assistant, built in** — Ask anything — current balance, bylaws, payment history, amenity bookings — and get an instant answer.

## Pricing

Simple, per-door pricing with no setup fees and no long-term contract. **$4 per door / month — flat** — the same per-door rate at every size; tiers unlock features, not a higher rate.
