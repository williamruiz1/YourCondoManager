## Pricing — Self-Managed Tier

Pricing is per association (community), flat monthly fee. Named tiers use community identity language, not capability language.

| Tier Name | Community Size | Monthly | Annual (10% off) |
|---|---|---|---|
| Small Community | Up to 30 units | $89/mo | $79/mo |
| Mid Community | 31–75 units | $139/mo | $119/mo |
| Large Community | 76–200 units | $199/mo | $169/mo |

**Benchmark competitor:** PayHOA
**Positioning rationale:** Priced above the low-end commodity tier ($49–$99) and well below enterprise tools ($275–$400+). Justified by stronger governance, document intelligence, and AI features that competitors lack.

## Pricing — Property Manager Tier

Pricing is per complex, graduated by complex size. PMs pay based on the unit count of each complex they manage.

| Tier | Complex Size | Price | Minimum Monthly |
|---|---|---|---|
| PM Tier 1 | Up to 30 units per complex | $30/mo per complex | $300/mo (10 complex floor) |
| PM Tier 2 | 31–75 units per complex | $50/mo per complex | $1,550/mo (natural floor at 31 units) |
| PM Tier 3 (Enterprise) | 75+ units per complex OR large portfolio volume | Contact us | — |

**Notes:**
- A PM managing complexes of mixed sizes pays the applicable tier rate per complex
- Tier 3 is a phone call — no self-serve signup
- Tier naming (currently Tier 1/2/3) is TBD — naming conversation pending

## Positioning — Two-Track Strategy

YCM serves two distinct buyer types with separate positioning:

### Track 1 — Self-Managed HOA Boards (Primary outreach target)
- Buyer: Volunteer board members who recently fired their property manager or are running on spreadsheets
- Pain: Operational chaos, no system of record, no audit trail
- Message: "You don't need a property manager. You need a better system."
- Geography: Connecticut (initial)
- Near-term target: 3 paying accounts
- ICP size: 15–200 units, self-managing, active board

### Track 2 — Property Managers
- Buyer: Small-to-mid property management firms
- Pain: Legacy software, manual reporting, tenant friction
- Message: "A modern platform built for how you actually work"
- Status: Secondary — not the focus of current outreach
