## Pricing — Graduated Scale
The $449/mo Property Manager tier price is a rounding artifact.
The intended price is $450/mo. However, the broader pricing
structure is a graduated scale by unit count — not flat tiers.
The $450 figure applies to the Property Manager tier at the
standard unit range. Self-Managed tier pricing starts at $99/mo
and scales by unit count. Full graduated scale TBD — needs a
dedicated pricing session before any external-facing update.

## Positioning — Two-Track Strategy
YCM serves two distinct buyer types with separate positioning:

**Track 1 — Self-Managed HOA Boards (Primary outreach target)**
- Buyer: Volunteer board members who recently fired their
  property manager or are running on spreadsheets
- Pain: Operational chaos, no system of record, no audit trail
- Message: "You don't need a property manager. You need a
  better system."
- Geography: Connecticut (initial)
- Target: 3 paying accounts

**Track 2 — Property Managers**
- Buyer: Small-to-mid property management firms
- Pain: Legacy software, manual reporting, tenant friction
- Message: "A modern platform built for how you actually work"
- Status: Secondary — not the focus of current outreach
