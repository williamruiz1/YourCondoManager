## Context

Breadcrumbs are the platform's wayfinding pattern beneath the sidebar and page title. 0.3 Q4 locked the shallow breadcrumb convention (`Association > Section > Page`, never >3 visible levels, labels human-readable and matching page titles, association context appears in association-scoped views, mobile may collapse or suppress — see `docs/projects/platform-overhaul/decisions/0.3-navigation-model.md:87-95` and acceptance criteria 16–20 at `docs/projects/platform-overhaul/decisions/0.3-navigation-model.md:167-172`).

0.1 locked the three Command Center labels and forbade the word "Dashboard" in the nav label, page title, or breadcrumb for `/app`, `/app/portfolio`, and `/app/operations/dashboard` (see `docs/projects/platform-overhaul/decisions/0.1-dashboard-resolution.md:119-146` and acceptance criteria 1, 5, 7 at `docs/projects/platform-overhaul/decisions/0.1-dashboard-resolution.md:165-172`).

0.2 locked the persona boundary matrix (`docs/projects/platform-overhaul/decisions/0.2-persona-map.md:114-129`) and must be respected when deciding whether Manager and Board Admin see the same breadcrumb chain at shared `/app` routes.

The primitive renderer at `client/src/components/breadcrumb-nav.tsx:9-37` accepts an arbitrary `{ label, href? }[]` array. It enforces no label conventions, no chain length, and no root label. Every page author has hand-built their own crumb chain, producing the inconsistencies enumerated below.

## Conflict Analysis

1. **"Dashboard" root is pervasive.** Eight rows still root the chain at "Dashboard" despite 0.1's "Home" rename. Until 1.3 locks a canonical root, every page author re-derives the root label from memory.
2. **Section-root ambiguity.** "Finance" vs "Financials" and "Board" vs functional zones (Governance, Communications, Elections) cannot be resolved inside 1.3 alone — this depends on 1.1 IA taxonomy. 1.3 must lock the *breadcrumb* label for each section, which may or may not equal the sidebar zone label.
3. **Association context absent on association-scoped views.** 0.3 AC #17 is not met on any current financial/operations/board crumb. Whether the "Association" crumb should be injected by the renderer, composed by the page, or sourced from a central catalog is a 3.4 decision, but 1.3 must decide *what label* appears in that slot (association display name? short code? "Sunny Shores HOA"?).
4. **Section hubs as crumbs.** Operations pages link their section crumb to `/app/operations/dashboard` (per 0.1 the page at that path is "Operations Overview"). Whether the hub gets its own crumb or the section root *is* the hub label is Q3.
5. **Renderer has no ceiling.** 0.3 AC #16 caps at three visible levels; the renderer enforces nothing. 1.3 surfaces this for 3.4 but does not implement the cap.
6. **Persona variance at shared routes.** Manager and Board Admin share `/app` per 0.2 + 0.3 Q3. Whether their breadcrumb chain is identical or persona-adapted (e.g., Board Admin's always-single-association scope may justify a shorter chain) is Q6.
7. **Parallel breadcrumb surfaces.** `association-context.tsx:672` and `financial-budgets.tsx:232` render breadcrumb-like UIs outside `BreadcrumbNav`. 1.3 must decide whether the catalog covers only `BreadcrumbNav` usage or all wayfinding surfaces.
