## Objective

Lock the definition, composition, URL convention, and naming convention for section hubs. Produce the canonical 13-section → five-zone mapping and designate exactly one hub page per zone (except Home, which is a dashboard). Hubs live at the zone root URL (`/app/financials`, `/app/operations`, `/app/governance`, `/app/communications`; portal equivalents `/portal/finances`, `/portal/requests`, `/portal/community`). Hub pages use the zone label alone as their display title — the word "Hub" never appears, and "Overview" is reserved for dashboard-type pages (preserving 0.1 AC 7 — Operations Overview at `/app/operations/dashboard` coexists with the new `/app/operations` hub).

---

## Acceptance Criteria (verbatim / tightly paraphrased from decision doc)

1. Every zone has exactly one hub page; hub pages are not workflow pages — no forms and no deep data tables; hubs are reachable directly from the sidebar zone label; hubs are not the same as the Home dashboard (Q1)
2. Zone title on hub matches sidebar label exactly; one-sentence zone description; sub-page link list present and complete; quick-action shortcuts do not exceed three; summary widget does not exceed one; no data table or workflow form (Q2)
3. All 13 sections are mapped to one of the five canonical zones; each zone has exactly one designated hub page; mapping table committed to the decision doc; no section silently dropped (Q3)
4. Each zone hub is accessible at its zone root URL; no hub lives at a sub-path of the zone root; Home and Owner Home are dashboards, not hubs, and are exempt from hub content rules (Q4)
5. Four hubs exist in `/app`: Financials, Operations, Governance, Communications; three hubs exist in `/portal`: My Finances, My Requests, My Community; `/app` and `/portal` are dashboards (Q5)
6. Hub sub-page lists are filtered by persona; a user never sees a link on a hub to a page they cannot access; hub filtering uses the same role-gating model as the sidebar (Q6)
7. Hub page `<h1>` matches the zone label exactly; no hub page title includes "Hub" or "Overview"; document title follows 1.4 format `Financials — YCM`, `Operations — YCM`, etc. (Q7)
8. `/app/operations` (hub, title "Operations", per Q7) and `/app/operations/dashboard` (Operations Overview, per 0.1 AC 7) coexist — the hub does not absorb the Overview page (Cross-Reference Notice)
