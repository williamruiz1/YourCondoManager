## Format

- `document.title` = `'{title} — YCM'` (em dash U+2014 with single space on each side; "YCM" uppercase). Locked by 1.4 Q1.
- Page `<h1>` matches the title field below exactly. Locked by 1.4 Q3.
- Hook signature: `useDocumentTitle(title: string)`. Locked by 1.4 Q2. Hook file lives at `client/src/hooks/useDocumentTitle.ts`.
- "Dashboard" is prohibited as a page title anywhere in the application. Locked by 1.4 Q5. No exceptions.
- Hub page titles use the **zone label alone** — no "Hub" suffix, no "Overview" suffix. Locked by 1.2 Q7.
- "Overview" is reserved for dashboard surfaces (e.g., Operations Overview) and for relocated workflow pages under a hub (`/app/governance/overview`, `/app/communications/overview`).
- Portal titles use first-person labels per 1.1 Q5 (My Finances, My Requests, My Community). Locked by 1.4 Q7.

## Unresolved / deferred (⚠️ flags)

The following routes/rows require founder clarification before Phase 3 wires the hook. All are conservative defaults that Phase 3 may ship safely; the ⚠️ marks the rows where the title may need to change when the upstream gap resolves.

1. ⚠️ **Row 24 `/app/operations/records` — "Operations Records"**. Handler is `DocumentsPage` with `typeFilter="Operations"`; this is a filtered-documents list, not an overview page. The title "Operations Records" is chosen over "Operations Documents" to avoid confusion with `/app/documents`. 1.4 does not explicitly name this route; derived from 1.1 Q3 Operations zone definition. Phase 3 may ship as "Operations Records"; founder may prefer "Operations Documents".

2. ⚠️ **Row 27 `/app/governance/elections/:id` — "Election Details"**. Current page renders dynamic election-specific title in body (see `1.4-page-title-consistency.md:21` note about election-detail / election-ballot being "dynamic data-driven titles"). `useDocumentTitle` accepts a `title: string` arg; the hook would need to receive a stable string. Two options: (a) stable title "Election Details" with dynamic election name only in `<h1>` — but this violates 1.4 Q3 (H1 must match title); or (b) the hook is called with the fetched election name, e.g., `useDocumentTitle(election?.title ?? "Election")`. Option (b) is preferred and matches the dynamic-title precedent at line 21; Phase 3 implementer decides per founder input.

3. ⚠️ **Rows 34/35/36 — Associations / Association Settings / New Association zone placement**. 1.1 Q3 does not explicitly name which zone these belong to. Placement in "Home" zone follows the 1.1 Q3 definition ("landing pages, summary dashboards, health indicators"); founder may relocate to a dedicated "Associations" super-zone if one is added.

4. ⚠️ **Row 42 `/app/documents` zone — "Home" vs. new zone**. Cross-zone document store. 1.1 Q3 does not place documents in any of the six zones; temporarily assigned to Home as a landing-style utility. Founder may carve out.

5. ⚠️ **Row P1 `/portal` Overview tab title — "Home" vs. "My Home"**. 1.1 Q5 lists the portal zone as "Home" (not "My Home"), but 1.4 Q7 says "portal page titles use first-person zone labels where appropriate (e.g., 'My Finances', 'My Requests')". The Overview tab could be titled "Home" (parity with `/app` Home, matches 1.1 Q5 zone name) or "My Home" (strict first-person). Default here: "Home". Founder call.

6. ⚠️ **Row P3 `/portal` Documents tab zone assignment**. Documents in the portal don't cleanly map to My Finances, My Requests, or My Community. Temporarily placed under "My Community". When 3.5 resolves Q1 (route depth), this title/zone will be revisited.

7. ⚠️ **Row P5 "Notices" title**. 1.4 Q7 requires first-person labels. "Notices" is non-first-person but matches user mental model (notices are pushed to owners, not authored by them). Alternative: "My Inbox" or "Association Notices". Founder call.

8. ⚠️ **Row P6 "Messages" title**. Tab label is "Communications" in the existing portal; renamed to "Messages" here to reflect thread-style DM UI and avoid parroting the `/app` "Communications" zone title. Founder may prefer "Messages" or "My Messages" or preserving "Communications".

9. ⚠️ **1.4 Q4 orphan renames** — "Association Overview", "Financial Overview", "Board Overview", "My Overview" have no concrete target route in the current 3.2 tree. 1.4 Q4 AC 3 requires agent to surface this; it is surfaced here. Phase 3 does **not** apply these titles anywhere — they remain unused strings until founder assigns target routes or formally deprecates the rows.

10. ⚠️ **Row 12 `/app/financial/reports` — "Financial Reports" vs. "Reports"**. In-page tab label in `App.tsx:147` is "Reports", but bare "Reports" would be ambiguous at the document.title level (there are also governance/operational reports). Disambiguated to "Financial Reports" here; founder may prefer bare "Reports" and accept the ambiguity.
