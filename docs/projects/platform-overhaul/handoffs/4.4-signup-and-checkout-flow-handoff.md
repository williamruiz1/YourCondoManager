## Scope summary

The signup + checkout flow that converts an anonymous visitor into an active operator persona (Manager, typically) with a paid subscription. Spans the public landing page, OAuth + email collection, plan selection, Stripe subscription creation, automatic operator-side login, and the onboarding banner that surfaces on the new operator's first Home visit.

This module is in IN SPEC (partial). The shipped tracks below cover Q1/Q3/Q4-resolved/Q7 P1; the remaining tracks (Q2/Q5/Q6 + Q4 Phase 0 migration) are tracked separately.

## Risks + mitigations

- **Risk:** the `req.login` auto-auth path silently fails in production due to session-store misconfiguration. **Mitigation:** magic-link fallback always emits, even on success, until analytics confirm zero fallback fires for one full notice cycle.
- **Risk:** OAuth email collision with an existing provider login locks the new signup out. **Mitigation:** Q7 reconciliation flow links the new subscription to the existing account; tested in `e2e/signup.spec.ts`.
