## Acceptance Criteria

### Q2 — Client route-guard policy
- Every `/app/platform/*`, `/app/admin/*`, `/app/ai/*` route in `client/src/App.tsx` wrapped by `<RouteGuard roles={["platform-admin"]}>`.
- No platform surface relies on server 403 alone for client-side gating.
- `canAccessWipRoute` (`client/src/lib/wip-features.ts:3-5`) consolidated into the centralized `<RouteGuard>` (2.3 Q9).
- No inline `adminRole === ...` ternaries remain at the route level for platform surfaces.

### Q3 — Server role-width policy
- All `/api/platform/*`, `/api/admin/*`, `/api/ai/ingestion/*` read and write handlers gate to `requireAdminRole(["platform-admin"])`.
- The widened reads catalogued in the decision doc's Cross-Reference Notices (`routes.ts:9676, 9730, 9767, 9818, 9856, 10030, 10039, 10047, 10077, 10161, 10169, 10208, 10219`, + all `/api/ai/ingestion/*` GET) narrow to `["platform-admin"]`.
- No read or write on these namespaces accepts `board-officer`, `assisted-board`, `pm-assistant`, `manager`, or `viewer`.

### Q4 — Tab-bar vs sidebar reconciliation
- `client/src/App.tsx:177-180` `platformSubPages` entries all gate to `roles: ["platform-admin"]`.
- `board-admin` (old role) entries on Admin Roadmap (`:177`) and Executive (`:178`) removed.
- Tab-bar role lists match the sidebar group `["platform-admin"]` gate on every platform surface.

### Q5 — Owner Portal launcher removal
- `client/src/components/app-sidebar.tsx:185` `/portal` launcher entry removed.
- `client/src/App.tsx:180` equivalent `platformSubPages` Owner Portal entry removed.
- No `/app/*` nav surface links to `/portal` after this PR.
- Platform Admin users accessing portal content do so via portal-access-id in a separate session — no operator-side shortcut.

### Q7 — Diagnostic surface strict narrow
- Every diagnostic/observability endpoint under `/api/platform/*` and `/api/admin/*` narrows to `requireAdminRole(["platform-admin"])`.
- No ad-hoc cross-role read exposure remains without a locked decision doc.
- Any future cross-role read grant cites 0.3 Q5 AC 22 + specific routes/roles in a new decision doc.
