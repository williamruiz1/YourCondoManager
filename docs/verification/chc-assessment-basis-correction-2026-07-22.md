## Outcome

- **$5,658.80 per installment-plan unit** is the financed scheduled-payment obligation. The schedule is approximately **$47.14 per month for 120 months**, with a small rounding or first/final-payment adjustment.

## Safe treatment in YCM

2. Present the current-period assessment installment separately from the total remaining assessment obligation.
3. Keep HOA dues, special-assessment principal, and assessment financing/interest distinguishable in statements and ledgers.

5. Do not certify the $9,810.63 as principal until a lender statement or amortization schedule identifies its date and basis.
6. Continue to hold disputed or low-confidence unit rows. This correction does not authorize a production balance load.
